Unite Punjab

Unite Punjab is a team who's prospective is work for the Punjab state of India. Unite Punjab team work to promote Punjabi's. Currently Unite Punjab is a Website where you can find almost every information a person want to know about "Punjab".
The main purpose of making this website and to have it a name "Unite Punjab" is because we want to make everything about Punjab combined up in a single place and demonstrate it to other world. We want to show other peoples of world that how our Punjab actually looks like.
Unite Punjab team work for every common person of the Punjab to make them famous around the world. Unite Punjab team work to make our Punjab Famous.

Punjab is one of the best Place of world where one can find peoples with very kind hearts, and provide best hospitality. Punjab is also one of the largest grain producer state of India.

Basically the word "Unite Punjab" is combination of two different words: "unite" meaning To bring together some action and "Punjab" which is a State of India. Hence you might now come to know the meaning of Unite Punjab: To bring together all the things of Punjab at single place. This is the work on which our Unite Punjab team works to make everything available to you very easily and in a simple form.
As we know our present generation is doing improvement in the Technology but they are going far away from their culture and their traditions, if this will continue for more half decade then the time will come when that generation will be so far from their culture and their traditions that no one will be available their to tell them about their past and their culture.
Therefore, Unite Punjab team takes an initiative to collect your all Traditions and our culture and show that a single platform as a "unitePunjab" website. We are also working for unite Punjab android application and iso application also which will be launch in coming months.
